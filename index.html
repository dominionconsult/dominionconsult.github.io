Who we are

Building inclusive futures

Dominion Consult is a Tanzanian consulting firm established in 2019, committed to evidence-based, participatory development across government, NGOs, private sector & academia.

Vision

To become a leading consultancy in Africa delivering transformative, equitable & sustainable social and economic development.

Mission

High-quality services in gender mainstreaming, research, training, coaching, data management & organizational strengthening to achieve sustainable outcomes.

Core Values

Professionalism · Inclusion · Integrity · Innovation · Evidence-based · Collaboration · Excellence · Respect for dignity.

Our approach

Participatory, gender-transformative & context-sensitive methodologies. We emphasize stakeholder ownership, ethical engagement, institutional sustainability, and practical implementation strategies for lasting impact.
